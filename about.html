About me

I transitioned from a career as a professional rider to front-end development,
bringing with me the discipline and attention to detail learned when working with horses.
My experience in the competitive world of professional riding taught me the importance of hard work and persistence, which I now apply to building user-friendly web interfaces.
As a front-end developer, I focus on creating functional and visually appealing websites, always striving to improve and learn.
This shift has allowed me to blend my passion for precision with a love for technology,
and I'm grateful for the opportunity to grow and contribute in this new field.
